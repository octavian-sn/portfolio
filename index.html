About Me

“Whether you think you can, or you think you can’t—you’re right.” - Henry Ford


It’s the mindset that’s been driving me for the past eight months, when I was no closer to web development, than knowing what ‘WWW’ is, to the point of building large scale apps with tools that I never knew they even existed.


I worked in the judicial system for almost five years, up until last year in May, when I decided to quit after a major burnout, that has left me with a complete disdain for everything the system is, and claims to stand for.


I needed purpose, so I turned to the one thing that I enjoyed most, computers. While I was studying UX Design, with the help of Google’s Coursera Certificate, I stumbled upon The Odin Project curriculum, which is a course made by developers for self learners.


Since then, I have been working my way through fundamentals, core concepts, design principles, advanced topics, rabbit-holes and whatnot… Pretty much anything, one would come across while making projects with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, all the while being guided by the curriculum.


For the first time ever, I genuinely enjoyed learning and was eager to discover more. It was like an unquenchable thirst for knowledge.


The entire experience has been more than a career shift for me, it helped me grow as a person altogether.Learning something new was no longer an ordeal, it was something that would help me instead of burden me, and also add value to me as a professional. I am constantly trying to push my limits and step out of the comfort zone to tackle challenging situations that would otherwise make me anxious.


Obviously, it was not all sunshine and rainbows, so when things got really rough, and I felt like quitting, dedication and persistence helped me push through, and add one more to the skill stack.


Currently, my main goal, is to learn as much as possible, and expand my skill set, to become proficient with back-end technologies too. I'm also hoping to find a team of talented and passionate people, along which I’m going to give my best efforts and dedication to achieve our common goals.
